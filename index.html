Joint Seller + Buyer | Carefully Worded
  • Joint letter from Seller + Buyer to ALL parents (Day 1 AM)
  • Message: “same teachers, same care, same safety standards”
  • Reassure: enrollment agreements unchanged & honored
  • Introduce new owner: background, vision, commitment to quality
  • Open door: parent Q&A session offered (Week 1)
  • No changes to tuition, schedules, or classroom assignments
STAFF & INTERNAL OPERATIONS
Teachers, Admin, Support Staff | To Be Scheduled Day 1–3
  • All-staff meeting: scheduled flexibly (task callout, not fixed time)
  • Seller introduces buyer; continuity & vision message
  • Open Q&A; “no surprises” policy declared
  • 1-on-1s with lead teachers & admin staff (Days 1–3)
  • HR systems: admin access, personnel files, benefits portal
  • IT systems: domain admin, parent portal, security cameras, alarms

Silent Transitions | Operations Never Pause
  • Director Integration

  • Insurance: D&O, E&O, property, liability policy updates
  • Landlord: lease assignment / change of control notice
  • Data protection: parent records, FERPA / state privacy compliance
  • Audit trail: pre/post close snapshot documentation
STABILIZE → DAYS 4–30
STABILIZATION (Days 4 – 30) Seller On-Site | Parent Confidence Building
  • Parent open-door Q&A session (Week 1)
  • Seller on-site: shadowing + introducing buyer to parents/staff (Days 1–20)
  • Lead teacher check-ins: retention pulse, satisfaction gauge
  • Full staff: benefits confirmed, payroll verified, culture pulse
  • Vendor continuity: food service, supplies, maintenance, cleaning
  • State childcare license: seamless owner change processing - OCFS Licensor already made aware
  • BoA account: full signing authority confirmed for buyer
  • Parent portal: new admin access live & tested
  • Emergency contacts: updated for new ownership
INTEGRATE → DAYS 20–60
INTEGRATION (Days 20 – 60) Seller Gradual Exit | Buyer Takes Reins
  • Seller transition: reduced on-site presence (Days 20–45)
  • Seller remote support: calls, introductions, guidance (Days 45–60)
  • Full parent roster: personal introductions complete
  • Operational rhythm: buyer fully hands-on daily management
  • Strategic planning: curriculum, enrichment programs, growth
  • Culture survey: staff sentiment & concerns check
  • Key supplier contracts: novation or renewal decisions
  • Financial controls: buyer solo signature authority activated
SELLER EXIT → DAY 60
SELLER EXIT (Day 60) Seller Departs | Full Ownership Transitioned
  • Day 60: seller transition support formally ends
  • Full buyer operational ownership: all systems, all decisions
  • Strategic planning: long-term vision & growth roadmap
  • Governance: board meeting rhythm established under new ownership
  • Earn-out compliance tracking (if applicable)
  • Long-term staff incentive plan: design & launch
NORMALIZATION → DAY 90+
NORMALIZATION (Day 90+) Full Ownership | Ongoing Operations
  • Full operational ownership: autonomous day-to-day management
  • Annual strategic planning cycle established
  • Governance: regular board meetings, annual reviews
  • Staff: long-term incentives, career pathing, professional development
  • Growth: enrollment expansion, new programs, facility improvements
NON-DISRUPTIVE TRANSFER — PRESCHOOL-SPECIFIC CORE PRINCIPLES
TEACHERS FIRST
All-staff meeting before parent letter. No layoff rumors. Classroom assignments unchanged.
PARENTS REASSURED
Joint seller+buyer letter. Same teachers, same care, same safety. Enrollment agreements honored fully.
CHILDREN NEVER NOTICE
Classroom routines unchanged. Same faces at drop-off & pick-up. No disruption to care quality.
SYSTEMS SEAMLESS
Parent portal stays live. Cameras work. BoA auto-pays uninterrupted. Payroll always funds on time.
GOVERNANCE SILENT
Childcare license change behind the scenes. Insurance: no gap coverage in-place. & invisible.
KEY RISKS & MITIGATIONS
Parent churn → Joint letter + same teachers + open-door Q&A
Teacher Retention → Day 1 face time + contracts honored
BoA banking freeze → Pre-cleared relationship manager + signing authority swap
System lockout → Parallel admin access Day 0 + credential handover checklist
State license Update → OCFS Licensor is in the loop and ready to make the change
Seller departure shock → 60-day graduated exit: on-site → remote → clean handoff
